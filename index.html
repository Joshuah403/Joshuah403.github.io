What is Finishing Touch

Finishing Touch will be a ‘cloud’ rendering service that will make 3D rendering and artwork creation a faster and more efficient process with the use of high performance CPUs and GPUs. Finishing Touch will support major 3D production software and make the process of uploading and rendering an easy and intuitive process. And with a simple pricing model of only 5 cents a minute (that's only 3 dollars an hour!).

 

And for the latest in AI development, at $15 an hour programmers can utilize the power of a Titan V one of the most powerful single GPUs ever made to execute and integrate their programs in order to speed up training time of neural networks and be on the forefront of software development without having to break the bank.

 

(for investors) By marketing to hobbyists and cash strapped people who can’t afford an expensive PC and Apple users who can’t sacrifice days of not being able to use their computer just to render a few seconds of animations we can gain hundreds of customers who are only just getting into 3D artwork, but aren’t industry professionals. The program will also be similarly useful for amateur and hobbyist programmers who can rent time for their programs by the hour, allowing for fast iteration of neural networks. The Titan V GPUs can have a performance gain of more the 80% when compared to a $700 consumer graphics card (1080ti).
